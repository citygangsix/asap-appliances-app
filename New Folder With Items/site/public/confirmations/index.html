Customer opt-in for service text messages.

This page documents how ASAP Appliance collects customer consent before sending SMS messages for appliance repair scheduling, appointment updates, estimates, invoices, technician arrival updates, and service follow-ups.

How consent is collected

Customer consent process

  • Customers enter their contact information when requesting appliance repair service.
  • The SMS consent checkbox is unchecked by default and must be selected by the customer.
  • The checkbox explains message purpose, frequency, carrier fees, STOP, HELP, and that consent is not required to buy service.
  • ASAP Appliance sends SMS only for service-related communications after opt-in.

Privacy policy

Privacy Policy

ASAP Appliance uses customer contact information to schedule appliance repair service, communicate appointment details, provide estimates and invoices, and follow up on service requests. Customer information is not sold or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes.

SMS opt-in records and service communication history may be retained to support customer service, compliance, and operational records.

SMS terms

SMS Terms

By opting in, customers agree to receive recurring service-related text messages from ASAP Appliance. Message frequency varies based on scheduling and service activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Customers can reply STOP to opt out at any time or HELP for help. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing appliance repair services.
