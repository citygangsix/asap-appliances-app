Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ASAP Appliance, operated by CASE-LESS INDUSTRIES LLC, collects and uses information when customers request appliance repair service through asapacboss.com, by the online service request form, by phone, by text, or through related service communications.

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect customer name, phone number, service address, appliance type, issue summary, preferred service timing, SMS consent status, email address if provided later, appointment details, technician notes, estimates, invoices, payment status, and communication history related to service requests.

How We Use Information

We use customer information to receive and log service requests, schedule service, confirm appointments, dispatch technicians, provide estimates and invoices, answer customer questions, follow up on service requests, improve operations, maintain records, and comply with legal or carrier requirements.

SMS Privacy and Mobile Opt-In Data

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in consent with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile numbers and SMS consent records are used for service-related communications from ASAP Appliance. We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate scheduling, messaging, billing, customer support, and related business systems. Those providers are not permitted to use mobile opt-in data for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS Program Details

  • Messages may include service appointment updates, technician ETA updates, missed-call follow-ups, job status updates, estimates, invoice or payment links when applicable, and customer support replies.
  • Message frequency varies based on the customer's service activity.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Customers can reply STOP to opt out at any time.
  • Customers can reply HELP for help or call 561-564-1545.

Data Retention and Security

We keep customer information for as long as reasonably needed for service, business, compliance, and recordkeeping purposes. We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect customer information, but no internet, phone, or messaging system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Customer Choices

Customers may request updates to their contact information or ask questions about this Privacy Policy by calling 561-564-1545. SMS recipients may opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP.
